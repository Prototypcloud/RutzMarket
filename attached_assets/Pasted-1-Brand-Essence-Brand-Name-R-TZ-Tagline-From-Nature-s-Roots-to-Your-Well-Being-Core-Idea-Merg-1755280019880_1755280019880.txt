1. Brand Essence

Brand Name: RÜTZ
Tagline: "From Nature’s Roots to Your Well-Being"
Core Idea: Merging the science of nature with heritage craftsmanship to deliver pure, potent botanical innovations.

2. Brand Story

RÜTZ is inspired by the German word Wurzeln (“roots”) and its deep connection to heritage, authenticity, and the source of life.

Born from the union of modern biotechnological precision and nature’s oldest wisdom, RÜTZ creates botanical extracts that nourish, protect, and restore. Our products are as pure as the roots they come from—crafted with care, backed by science, and inspired by nature’s timeless cycles.

3. Brand Personality

Trustworthy & Scientific – grounded in rigorous testing, certifications, and transparency.

Premium & Minimalist – a sleek, modern presentation with an emphasis on quality.

Natural & Authentic – rooted in the earth, sustainable sourcing, and clean production.

Global & Sophisticated – resonates in both German and English markets.

4. Brand Values

Purity – Only the cleanest, most potent extracts.

Sustainability – Ethical harvesting and eco-conscious packaging.

Innovation – Biotechnological advancements in plant-based health solutions.

Transparency – Clear origin, processing, and efficacy information.

Heritage – Inspired by European botanical traditions.

5. Visual Identity
Logo Concept

Modern sans-serif (geometric but warm, e.g., Avenir Next or Futura PT).

The “Ü” stylized as a leaf-shaped umlaut to visually connect to the botanical theme.

Style: Clean, minimal, with space to breathe—reflecting purity.

Variations:

Horizontal lockup with tagline.

Monogram version: “RÜ” with leaf accent for packaging icons.
